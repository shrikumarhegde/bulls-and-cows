Bulls and Cows

Welcome to Bulls and Cows Game!

Browser generates a random sequence of 4 (four) unique digits from 0 to 9. The goal of the game is to guess the generated sequence

Rules

  • On each step, game makes a guess: enter 4 unique digits from 0 to 9 in any order.
  • Game will evaluate a guess and give a response with number of bulls and cows. Bull means you have guessed a digit and its position in a sequence correctly. Cow means you have guessed a digit but put it in a wrong place
  • Guess the number sequence and win the game!
